Registration process

Registration is handled in four steps. Your place is only secured after payment is received and confirmed.

1) Application

Submit application form

Fill in the application form with your details. If the programme reaches capacity, applications will be queued in a waiting list.

2) Pre‑confirmation

After submitting the form, you will receive a confirmation that your application has been received. The organisation may contact you if additional information is needed.

3) Payment

Make a bank transfer to the following bank account:

URJC IBAN: ES86 0049 1892 6221 1054 6502

Then email the payment proof to irobocity2030@gmail.com.

4) Attendance confirmation

Once payment is verified, you will receive the final confirmation to attend. Participants who have not paid by the payment deadline will not be confirmed, and their place will be reassigned to the next person on the waiting list.
